Details about PRISCA

Aadhavan Candies was started in the year 2017 with a long-term goal of supplying unique, delicious and nutritious peanut crunchy bars to the society. It was initiated as a miniature distribution company with an employee count of about five as a micro marketing capacity.

In 2019, the company grew to the level of supplying quality raw materials including peanut and jaggery to some manufacturing units producing peanut candies in a large chunk. In the process of supplying the raw material, the employee count was raised ten.
